Everyone has the potential
to create new positive habits.

Unfortunately, most people fail to do so because they lack organization and support to overcome their tendency to choose short-term rewards.

​​Exercise, in particular, is among the most popular habits people want to create, but studies show 50% give up in 6 weeks or less.

Luckily, new technologies allow us to create tools to help people with the organization and support they need.

An app to make exercise a habit.

RocketJourney uses game mechanics to persuade people to not lose their exercise streak.

Game Mechanics

  • - Increase your streak every week by exercising 3+ days per week.
  • - If you don’t, you lose your streak, and have to start over from zero.
  • - Unless, you have saved enough cheat days. The more often you exercise, the more cheat days you earn.

Whether a person exercises at a fitness center, at home, or outdoors, there are ways to verify his or her activities to make progress in the game.

A Willpower Competition.

People are more likely to stick to their resolutions when they are held accountable.

In RocketJourney, friends can form groups to compete and support each other.

Competing in RocketJourney has become very popular because it is hard to cheat.

On the other side of the table there’s Control Center

Control Center is a Cloud Based Software that facilitates member retention management by helping a club better understand its members and take action to improve their experience.

Control Center integrates with RocketJourney to analyse members’ activity involvement and social support network. It uses this data to give each member a retention score that reflects the probability he or she will continue being a member in the future.

Control Center suggests actions to incentivize members to meet other members and try new things.

  • Facilitating member-to-member connections is crucial for creating a sense of belonging, and thus, increase member retention.
  • Retention rates are higher for those who use multiple services, as well as those who enter group classes.

Customer Acquisition

Since anyone can download the App for free, we can make first contact with potential customers via their own members. How? By making it very simple for anyone to solicit their club to subscribe.

So if we were looking to close a sale with a specific club, we could run a marketing campaign targeted at that club’s members.

As more members of that club download the app, more will solicit their club to subscribe in order for them to be able to advance in the game by checking in.

Customer Retention

Since Control Center integrates with RocketJourney, affiliated clubs have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to all their members, so they can have more and better information to act upon.

As more members of affiliated clubs use RocketJourney, churn becomes less likely, since unsubscribing would mean taking away value from their own members.

Our vision

Become the world’s go-to platform for turning exercise into a habit.

We have a few things running in our favor:

1 By partnering with tracking apps and devices, we can target millions of people already using these products, and invite them to join the game.

2 Our customers have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to their own clients. Trainers and staff share the app with members, and we work together to run email marketing and social media campaigns to spread the word.
