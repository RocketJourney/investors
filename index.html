The Opportunity

Everyone has the potential to
make exercise a habit.

Unfortunately, most people fail because they
lack organization and support.

Studies show 50% of people stop exercising after 6 weeks or less.

Getting motivated to exercise is tricky because the short term reward is too small compared to the effort required; therefore, if people rely on willpower alone, they will likely choose to not exercise today.

Most people don’t track their exercise habit.

There are many products that help people track workout metrics like calories burned, distance ran, etc.

But what’s the point of having the best workout tracker if people can’t get themselves to work out in the first place? If the goal is to make exercise a habit, then exercise habit is what people need to track.

The Product

RocketJourney is an app with a gameful design to
help users track and sustain their exercise habit.

Users just have to follow 3 simple rules:

  • The goal is to exercise at least 3 days per week to increase your streak.
  • You have to get your 3 days of exercise before Sunday night. If not, you’ll lose your streak.
  • You can earn cheat days to maintain your streak on lazy weeks.
  • Notes:
    (1) (3) provide positive reinforcement,
    (2) triggers a sense of loss aversion.

Users automatically advance in the game every day they exercise.

RocketJourney ensures a good balance between verification simplicity and cheating difficulty. To verify their exercise activities, users have to connect once with one or more verification sources, such as: tracking apps, devices, check-in softwares, beacons, etc. From that day forward, users automatically advance in the game by using that verification source and meeting the required criteria.

Users can create groups to motivate each other.

Since keeping an exercise streak is something that applies to everybody, all users can compare themselves to each other, regardless of fitness level or preferred type of exercise. This attracts groups of friends to create groups in RocketJourney to compete on who can sustain the longest streak, keep track of challenges and bets, or simply support each other.

Go-to-Market Stratey

The best way to distribute this product is
to partner with fitness clubs.

We offer the product, they promote it.

There are 200,000+ fitness clubs serving 162+ million members worldwide.

The U.S. has the largest number of fitness club members; 57.2 million Americans have a fitness club membership. Other interesting markets in terms of fitness club members include: Germany (10M), U.K. (9.7M), Brazil (9.6M), Canada (5.6M), France (5.4M), Italy (5.2M), Spain (5M), Japan (4.1M), and Mexico (4.1M).

RocketJourney helps fitness clubs increase member retention.

Every year, fitness clubs lose on average 40% of their members. Most members stop renewing their memberships because they lose motivation and stop visiting their club frequently enough to justify the expense. RocketJourney motivates members to continue visiting longer periods of time, and therefore continue paying their memberships.

Clubs can integrate to RocketJourney for free.

When a club integrates, its members become able to automatically advance in the RocketJourney game every day they attend the club. To integrate, a club has two options:

Integrate its software with the RocketJourney API.

Members have to:
Check in like they normally do.

Ideal for:
Clubs with automated check-in system.

Place a bluetooth beacon inside the club.

Members have to:
Turn on bluetooth.

Ideal for:
Clubs without automated check-in system.

Once integrated, fitness clubs promote RocketJourney to their members.

Clubs have great ability to promote RocketJourney because they collect members’ email addresses and contact information. Additionally, clubs have an incentive to promote RocketJourney on an ongoing basis to every new member because the first weeks of membership usage are key to long-term retention.

Users help us convince their clubs to integrate to RocketJourney.

In RocketJourney, users can request their clubs to integrate. We use these requests as leverage to approach and convince these clubs to integrate. So, if we want to motivate a specific club to integrate, we can run user acquisition campaigns targeted at its members to get a few of them to download RocketJourney and request their club to integrate.

The intense competition of the fitness club industry plays in our favor.

The fitness club industry is very competitive because the main reason people choose one club over another is closeness to home/work. This means that if one or more clubs in a specific region integrate to RocketJourney, other clubs nearby will be motivated to integrate to avoid lagging behind their competitors.

As our network of clubs grows, more and more clubs will want to integrate.

Once our network of clubs reaches a tipping point in a region, it will no longer make sense for clubs in that region to not integrate because they will realize that a significant number of potential customers prefer to join a club that allows them to advance in the RocketJourney game. Not to mention that they could also start losing members for the same reason.

The Bussiness Model

People will be able to buy and sell exercise plans.

We’ll charge a 20% fee for every transaction.

Many people don’t know what to do for exercise.

RocketJourney has proven to be very effective in motivating people to visit their club more often. However, many people don’t really know what to do once they get there, so they do the same routines they once learned, again and again. This lack of clarity and variety are barriers to making progress and staying motivated.

Providers of exercise plans are struggling to find clients.

Many trainers and fitness influencers are already creating and selling exercise plans, mostly to people they meet at their fitness clubs. Some of them have tried distributing their plans online, but have failed to get clients outside their inner circles.

We will offer a separate application to create and sell exercise plans.

Providers of exercise plans will be able to create, publish, and sell their plans to RocketJourney users.

For every plan they publish, they will have to fill a questionnaire to help users filter out and find the plans that match their goals and preferences. Providers will receive reviews and completion stats of their plans.

RocketJourney will turn exercise plans into time-limited gamified journeys to motivate users to follow them through.

RocketJourney will gamify plans to motivate users to complete them. First of all, users will need to verify they exercised to make progress in the plan. Second, the plan will automatically adapt to the user’s completion progress. And third, the plan will disappear after a deadline, giving users a sense of urgency to complete it.

Paid digital fitness is growing by 400% year on year.

The top providers of online exercise plans include: Beachbody, Les Mills, Yogaia, Wexer, and Fitness on Demand. Companies like these, as well as independent trainers and fitness influencers can benefit from distributing their plans via RocketJourney. But to get them on board, we must first build a strong user base that will buy the plans; otherwise, we will encounter a chicken and egg problem.

The largest economic opportunity is in this model.

We don’t rule out the possibility of having a traditional B2C monetization model, such as advertising or e-commerce. There are B2B opportunities as well; for example, we could sell premium features and services to fitness clubs, or we could sell a web product for managing employee wellness to any type of business.

Nevertheless, the largest economic opportunity is in this model. There’s a rapidly growing market that is willing to pay for online exercise plans. We just passed 2.5bn smartphone users on earth and are heading towards 5bn, 6 out of 10 have downloaded a health-related app, and fitness is the world’s biggest sport.

Our vision

Become the world’s go-to platform for turning exercise into a habit.

We have a few things running in our favor:

1 By partnering with tracking apps and devices, we can target millions of people already using these products, and invite them to join the game.

2 Our customers have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to their own clients. Trainers and staff share the app with members, and we work together to run email marketing and social media campaigns to spread the word.

3 and most importantly, unlike other fitness apps, RocketJourney provides all users with one main metric to compare themselves against, and that is, consistency. This means that teams can be made up of people with different workout preferences and fitness levels.
