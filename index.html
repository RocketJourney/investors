Every year, the average fitness club
loses 40% of its members 1

Member retention is the Achilles Heel of the fitness club industry. This comes as no surprise considering how difficult it is to turn exercise into a habit.

More than 50% of people can’t stay on an exercise streak for more than 6 weeks in a row. 2

If members fail to develop a habit of regular exercise, they won’t attend their club frequently enough, and consequently, they will stop seeing the value of their memberships.

Today, the fitness club industry is becoming more competitive, and the cost of acquiring a customer is on the rise. Club owners can’t keep outrunning the retention challenge by simply selling more memberships than they lose.

Increasing member retention is no longer optional...

RocketJourney, a mobile app that turns exercise into a game.

The name of the game is to exercise for as many weeks in a row as you can.


When you check in at your club, RocketJourney automatically counts your attendance as a day of exercise, and just like that, you advance in the game.

You can also advance in the game by exercising outdoors or at home. You just have to track your workouts with one of our partner apps or devices:

The only rule is to exercise at least 3 days a week. Otherwise you lose your streak and have to start over.


Cheat days can be used to maintain your streak in case of vacations, illness, etc. The more you exercise, the more cheat days you earn.

Teams, our Accountability Tool

RocketJourney is free, everyone can join the game.


This allows users to team up with their friends and family, even if they don’t go to the same club, or even if they don’t go to a club at all.

Teaming up provides a powerful combination of support and accountability which significantly increases the chances of sticking to one’s commitments.

Shame Aversion Incentive:
If you become inactive, you are put on the wall of shame


Every activity generates a workout summary where users can add their routine, progress pictures, or whatever comes to mind.

RocketJourney offers in-app branding to affiliated clubs. In addition, users can share their workout summaries in social networks, promoting their club’s brand in an organic fashion.

On the other side of the table there’s Control Center

Control Center is a Cloud Based Software that facilitates member retention management by helping a club better understand its members and take action to improve their experience.

Control Center integrates with RocketJourney to analyse members’ activity involvement and social support network. It uses this data to give each member a retention score that reflects the probability he or she will continue being a member in the future.

Control Center suggests actions to incentivize members to meet other members and try new things.

  • Facilitating member-to-member connections is crucial for creating a sense of belonging, and thus, increase member retention.
  • Retention rates are higher for those who use multiple services, as well as those who enter group classes.

Customer Acquisition

Since anyone can download the App for free, we can make first contact with potential customers via their own members. How? By making it very simple for anyone to solicit their club to subscribe.

So if we were looking to close a sale with a specific club, we could run a marketing campaign targeted at that club’s members.

As more members of that club download the app, more will solicit their club to subscribe in order for them to be able to advance in the game by checking in.

Customer Retention

Since Control Center integrates with RocketJourney, affiliated clubs have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to all their members, so they can have more and better information to act upon.

As more members of affiliated clubs use RocketJourney, churn becomes less likely, since unsubscribing would mean taking away value from their own members.

Our vision

Become the world’s go-to platform for turning exercise into a habit.

We have a few things running in our favor:

1 By partnering with tracking apps and devices, we can target millions of people already using these products, and invite them to join the game.

2 Our customers have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to their own clients. Trainers and staff share the app with members, and we work together to run email marketing and social media campaigns to spread the word.
