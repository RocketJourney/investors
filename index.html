Everyone has the potential
to create new positive habits.

Unfortunately, most people fail to sustain a new behavior long enough to make it a habit because they lack organization and support.

Making exercise a habit is particularly problematic.

When people set out to make exercise a habit, they usually start exercising 3 or more days on week #1, then again on week #2; but before long, they start exercising less frequently each week, until completely falling off the wagon. Studies show 50% of people give up in 6 weeks or less.

Getting motivated to exercise is tricky because the short term reward is too small compared to the effort required; therefore, if people rely on willpower alone, which is an inconsistent source of motivation, they will likely choose to not exercise today.

Luckily, new technologies allow us to design new types of tools to help people get the organization and support they need. For this reason, we created RocketJourney.

RocketJourney helps using game mechanics.

RocketJourney is an app designed to help people become more aware of their exercise habit and avoid falling off the wagon. Users just have to follow these rules:

Rules of the Game

  • Exercise 3 or more days per week to increase your week streak.
  • If you don’t, you lose your week streak, and have to start over.
  • Unless, you have saved enough cheat days. The more often you exercise, the more cheat days you earn.
  • Notes:
    (1) (3) provide positive reinforcement,
    (2) triggers a sense of loss aversion.

All types of workouts are verified automatically.

The RocketJourney API allows fitness club members to automatically advance in the game every day they check in like they normally do when they enter their club.

RocketJourney also integrates with apps and devices to allow people who exercise outdoors or at home to automatically advance in the game every day they track a workout that meets the required criteria.

Friends can compete, regardless of fitness level or type of workout.

RocketJourney is an effective accountability tool because advancing in the game is 1) automatic, 2) hard to cheat, and 3) universally comparable.

This attracts friends and families to form groups to support each other and compete on who can sustain the longest streak.

To distribute this product, the highest leverage point is to partner with fitness centers.

We offer the product, they promote it.

RocketJourney helps fitness clubs increase member retention by motivating members to continue visiting longer periods of time.

Member retention is the achilles heel of the fitness club industry. Every year, fitness clubs lose on average 40% of their members.

Most members stop renewing their memberships because they lose motivation and stop visiting their club frequently enough to justify the expense.

Fitness clubs can integrate with RocketJourney online, for free.

When a club integrates, its members become able to automatically advance in the game every day they check in like they normally do when they enter their club.

To integrate, a club has to communicate with the RocketJourney API to 1) validate that a RocketJourney user is a member of the club, and 2) send check-in data in real time.

If a club uses a club management software (CMS) that's already integrated with RocketJourney, then everything can be set up with just a few clicks.

Fortunately, the biggest CMS companies service thousands of fitness clubs, and are open to integrations with products like RocketJourney if one or more clients ask for it.

via API

Since anyone can download the App for free, we can make first contact with potential customers via their own members. How? By making it very simple for anyone to solicit their club to subscribe.

So if we were looking to close a sale with a specific club, we could run a marketing campaign targeted at that club’s members.

As more members of that club download the app, more will solicit their club to subscribe in order for them to be able to advance in the game by checking in.

via CMS

Since Control Center integrates with RocketJourney, affiliated clubs have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to all their members, so they can have more and better information to act upon.

As more members of affiliated clubs use RocketJourney, churn becomes less likely, since unsubscribing would mean taking away value from their own members.

RocketJourney users help us convince clubs to integrate.

Users who are members of a club (club users) can easily request their club to integrate. We use these requests as leverage to convince those clubs to integrate.

We can increase the number of requests for a specific club by running user acquisition campaigns targeted at that club’s members.

Users who are not members of a club (non-club users) can see a list of integrated clubs, ordered by proximity, and solicit membership information. This motivates clubs to integrate if they believe their target market is using RocketJourney.

RocketJourney’s user base growth is powered by viral user acquisition.

When a club integrates and promotes RocketJourney to its members, many of them sign up and invite their friends to form groups; these new users then invite other new users, which motivates more clubs to integrate.

**Images: Cycle Image**

To accelerate the cycle:
We have to device incentives to motivate new users to form groups as soon as soon as possible after they sign up.

To strengthen the cycle:
We have to increase the number of new users that each user brings onboard by:

1) Making exercise verification more accessible to more people, by integrating with as many clubs, apps, and devices as we can, as well as building our own tracking features, and

2) Consistently improving our core product, especially our multiplayer features.

Our vision

Become the world’s go-to platform for turning exercise into a habit.

We have a few things running in our favor:

1 By partnering with tracking apps and devices, we can target millions of people already using these products, and invite them to join the game.

2 Our customers have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to their own clients. Trainers and staff share the app with members, and we work together to run email marketing and social media campaigns to spread the word.

3 and most importantly, unlike other fitness apps, RocketJourney provides all users with one main metric to compare themselves against, and that is, consistency. This means that teams can be made up of people with different workout preferences and fitness levels. This increases the viral coefficient of RocketJourney, and ultimately, the development of network effects.
