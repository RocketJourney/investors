Everyone has the potential
to create new positive habits.

Unfortunately, most people fail to sustain a new behavior long enough to make it a habit because they lack organization and support.

This problem is particularly evident in exercise.

Excercise is among the most popular habits people want to create, but studies show 50% give up in 6 weeks or less.

When people set out to make exercise a habit, they usually start exercising 3 or more days on week #1, then again on week #2, and so on; but before long, they start exercising less frequently each week, until quitting altogether.

People set themselves up for failure because they rely exclusively on willpower, so when the time of the day to make a choice comes, people are more likely to choose to not exercise.

Luckily, new technologies allow us to design new types of tools to help people get the organization and support they need.

RocketJourney is an app that uses game mechanics to help people become more aware of their own exercise consistency and avoid falling off the wagon.

  • Exercise 3 or more days per week to increase your week streak.
  • If you don’t, you lose your week streak, and have to start over.
  • Unless, you have saved enough cheat days. The more often you exercise, the more cheat days you earn.
  • Notes:
    (1) (3) provide positive reinforcement,
    (2) triggers a sense of loss aversion.

Whether a person exercises at a fitness center, at home, or outdoors, there are ways to verify his or her activities to make progress in the game.

The RocketJourney API allows fitness centers to integrate their check-in software online. This way, they enable their members to automatically advance in the game every day they attend.

RocketJourney also integrates with the most popular tracking apps and devices. Users have to meet specific criteria in order to advance in the game.

Since sustaining an exercise streak applies to everyone, all friends can compete in RocketJourney, regardless of fitness level or preferred activity.

RocketJourney is a very effective accountability tool because advancing in the game is 1) automatic, 2) hard to cheat, and 3) universally comparable.

This attracts friends and family to form groups to support each other and compete on who can sustain the longest streak.

To distribute this product, the highest leverage point is to partner with fitness centers.

And we have a evil master plan to conquer them.

Every year, fitness clubs lose on average 40% of their members.

Attendance is the variable that best predicts retention. Most members stop renewing their memberships because they lose motivation and stop visiting their club frequently enough.

RocketJourney helps fitness clubs increase retention by motivating members to continue visiting longer periods of time than they would without RocketJourney.

Customer Acquisition

Since anyone can download the App for free, we can make first contact with potential customers via their own members. How? By making it very simple for anyone to solicit their club to subscribe.

So if we were looking to close a sale with a specific club, we could run a marketing campaign targeted at that club’s members.

As more members of that club download the app, more will solicit their club to subscribe in order for them to be able to advance in the game by checking in.

Customer Retention

Since Control Center integrates with RocketJourney, affiliated clubs have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to all their members, so they can have more and better information to act upon.

As more members of affiliated clubs use RocketJourney, churn becomes less likely, since unsubscribing would mean taking away value from their own members.

Our vision

Become the world’s go-to platform for turning exercise into a habit.

We have a few things running in our favor:

1 By partnering with tracking apps and devices, we can target millions of people already using these products, and invite them to join the game.

2 Our customers have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to their own clients. Trainers and staff share the app with members, and we work together to run email marketing and social media campaigns to spread the word.

3 and most importantly, unlike other fitness apps, RocketJourney provides all users with one main metric to compare themselves against, and that is, consistency. This means that teams can be made up of people with different workout preferences and fitness levels. This increases the viral coefficient of RocketJourney, and ultimately, the development of network effects.
