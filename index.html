Everyone has the potential
to create new positive habits.

Unfortunately, most people fail to sustain a new behavior long enough to make it a habit because they lack organization and support.

Making exercise a habit is particularly problematic.

When people set out to make exercise a habit, they usually start exercising 3 or more days on week #1, then again on week #2; but before long, they start exercising less frequently each week, until completely falling off the wagon. Studies show 50% of people give up in 6 weeks or less.

Getting motivated to exercise is tricky because the short term reward is too small compared to the effort required; therefore, if people rely on willpower alone, which is an inconsistent source of motivation, they will likely choose to not exercise today.

Luckily, new technologies allow us to design new types of tools to help people get the organization and support they need. For this reason, we created RocketJourney.

RocketJourney is an app that uses gamification to help users track and sustain their exercise habit.

Users just have to follow 3 simple rules:

  • The goal is to exercise at least 3 days per week to increase your streak.
  • You have to get your 3 days of exercise before Sunday night. If not, you’ll lose your streak.
  • You can earn cheat days to maintain your streak on lazy weeks.
  • Notes:
    (1) (3) provide positive reinforcement,
    (2) triggers a sense of loss aversion.

Gamification

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gamification is the application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.

A collection of research on gamification shows that a majority of studies on gamification find it has positive effects on individuals.

Users automatically advance in the game every day they exercise.

To verify their exercise activities, users have to connect once with 1 or more verification sources, such as: tracking apps, devices, check-in systems, etc. From that day forward, users automatically advance in the game by using that verification source and meeting the required criteria.

RocketJourney ensures a good balance between verification simplicity and cheating difficulty.

Users can create groups to motivate each other.

RocketJourney users can compare themselves to each other, regardless of fitness level or preferred type of exercise, because keeping an exercise streak is something that applies to everybody.

This attracts groups of friends to create groups in RocketJourney to compete on who can sustain the longest streak, keep track of challenges and bets, or simply support each other.

Group members can communicate with each other and get notified when they exercise. More importantly, they can see each other’s status in the game, which motivates them to not lose their streak, because if they do, all group members will know, and that is meaningful in RocketJourney because the game is very straightforward and hard to cheat.

And the best way to distribute this product is to partner with fitness clubs.

We offer the product, they promote it.

RocketJourney helps fitness clubs increase member retention by motivating members to continue visiting longer periods of time.

Retaining members is the achilles heel of the fitness club industry. Every year, fitness clubs lose on average 40% of their members. Most members stop renewing their memberships because they lose motivation and stop visiting their club frequently enough to justify the expense.

Clubs can integrate to RocketJourney for free.

When a club integrates to RocketJourney, its members become able to connect to their club in RocketJourney and automatically advance in the game every day they attend the club.

To integrate to RocketJourney, clubs have two options:

  1. 1) Integrate their Club Management Software (CMS) with the RocketJourney API, or
  2. 2) Place bluetooth beacons inside the club

**Image: Connection Methods Table**

Users help us convince their clubs to integrate.

In the app, users can see a list of integrated clubs and request their club to integrate if it hasn’t. We use these requests as leverage to convince these clubs to integrate.

*Image: User Emails* , *Image: Gyms List with Request button*

Clubs promote RocketJourney to their members, then members invite their friends to join. As more users join, it becomes more attractive for clubs to integrate.

When a club integrates to RocketJourney, it promotes the app to all members via email & social media campaigns. They also continuously promote the app to every new members, and are free to launch challenges and competitions.

Since RocketJourney is an open app, members can invite their friends and families even when they don’t exercise at the same club or at any club at all.

Our vision

Become the world’s go-to platform for turning exercise into a habit.

We have a few things running in our favor:

1 By partnering with tracking apps and devices, we can target millions of people already using these products, and invite them to join the game.

2 Our customers have an incentive to promote RocketJourney to their own clients. Trainers and staff share the app with members, and we work together to run email marketing and social media campaigns to spread the word.

3 and most importantly, unlike other fitness apps, RocketJourney provides all users with one main metric to compare themselves against, and that is, consistency. This means that teams can be made up of people with different workout preferences and fitness levels. This increases the viral coefficient of RocketJourney, and ultimately, the development of network effects.
