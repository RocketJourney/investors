The Opportunity

Everyone has the potential to make exercise a habit.

Sadly, most people fail because they don’t measure their habit and aren’t held accountable.

Studies show 50% of people stop exercising after 6 weeks or less.

Getting motivated to exercise is tricky because the short term reward is too small compared to the effort required; therefore, if people rely on willpower alone, they will likely choose to not exercise today.


* fix image zoom *

People fail because they don’t measure their exercise habit.

Exercise trackers measure things like heart rate and calories burned, but fail to motivate people to exercise in the first place. Making exercise a habit should be Goal #1, and exercise habit the thing to measure.

People fail because they aren’t held accountable.

Being held accountable is highly motivating, but holding people accountable is complicated because results have to constantly be shared and compared to some (usually unclear) criteria that determines success.

The Product

RocketJourney is an app with a gameful design to help users measure and sustain their exercise habit.

Users just have to follow 3 simple rules:

  • The goal is to exercise at least 3 days per week to increase your streak.
  • You have to get your 3 days of exercise before Sunday night. If not, you’ll lose your streak.
  • You can earn cheat days to maintain your streak on lazy weeks.
  • Incentives:
    (1) (3) provide positive reinforcement,
    (2) triggers a sense of loss aversion.

* is this final? kinda ugly *

Users automatically advance in the game every day they exercise.

To verify their exercise activities, users have to connect once with one or more verification sources, such as tracking apps/devices and check-in softwares/beacons. From that day forward, users automatically advance in the game by using that verification source and meeting the required criteria.

Users can create groups to hold each other accountable..

Since keeping an exercise streak is something that applies to everybody, all users can compare themselves to each other, regardless of fitness level or preferred type of exercise. This attracts groups of friends to create groups in RocketJourney to compete on who can sustain the longest streak, keep track of challenges and bets, or simply hold each other accountable.

Go-to-Market Stratey

The best way to distribute this product is to partner with fitness clubs.

We offer the product, they promote it.

There are 200,000+ fitness clubs serving 162+ million members worldwide.

1 out of every 5 Americans have a fitness club membership (57M people). Other interesting markets include: Germany (10M), U.K. (9.7M), Brazil (9.6M), Canada (5.6M), France (5.4M), Italy (5.2M), Spain (5M), Japan (4.1M), and Mexico (4.1M).

RocketJourney helps fitness clubs increase member retention.

Every year, fitness clubs lose on average 40% of their members. Most members stop renewing their memberships because they lose motivation and stop visiting their club frequently enough to justify the expense. RocketJourney motivates members to continue visiting longer periods of time, and therefore continue paying their memberships.

Clubs can integrate to RocketJourney for free.

When a club integrates, its members become able to automatically advance in the RocketJourney game every day they attend the club. To integrate, a club has two options:

Integrate its software with the RocketJourney API.

Members have to:
Check in like they normally do.

Ideal for:
Clubs with automated check-in system.

Place a bluetooth beacon inside the club.

Members have to:
Turn on bluetooth.

Ideal for:
Clubs without automated check-in system.

Once integrated, fitness clubs promote RocketJourney to their members.

Clubs have great ability to promote RocketJourney because they collect members’ email addresses and contact information. Additionally, clubs have an incentive to promote RocketJourney on an ongoing basis to every new member because the first weeks of membership usage are key to long-term retention.

Users help us convince their clubs to integrate to RocketJourney.

In RocketJourney, users can request their clubs to integrate. We use these requests as leverage to approach and convince these clubs to integrate. This means we could motivate a specific club to integrate, we can run user acquisition campaigns targeted at its members to get a few of them to download RocketJourney and request their club to integrate.

The goal is to develop a worldwide network of fitness clubs.

Our goal is to partner with as many fitness clubs as we can to acquire as many users as we can, until we hit a tipping point in which clubs that haven’t integrated realize they have to integrate because a lot of potential members prefer to join a club that allows them to advance in the RocketJourney game.

The Business Model

We will sell premium functionality to fitness clubs.

Fitness clubs will pay a subscription to get access to the premium features of Control Center.

Control Center is a freemium web application we offer to fitness clubs.

The free version of Control Center helps clubs integrate to RocketJourney, promote the app to members, and offer support. In other words, it facilitates the adoption and proper functioning of RocketJourney at the club.

The premium version of Control Center will help clubs manage member retention more effectively and offer exercise plans to members via RocketJourney.

Clubs will be able to manage member retention more effectively.

The premium version of Control Center will calculate the probability that members will continue paying their memberships based on attendance patterns, exercise activities, Net Promoter Score, and other data obtained via RocketJourney. It will also provide prescriptive tips to help managers prevent high-risk members from cancelling.

Our Competition

RocketJourney is first in a new category.

We offer fitness club members a unique experience that no other app offers.

We compete indirectly with White Label Apps..

White label app providers offer custom branded apps, usually focused on membership management and class scheduling features. On the other hand, RocketJourney offers an open, centralized app, focused on gameful motivational features.

This means that fitness clubs don’t have to choose between paying for their own branded app and integrating to RocketJourney for free; they can do both and offer both products to members.
